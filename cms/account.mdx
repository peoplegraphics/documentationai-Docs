## Creating an account

On the **Create your account** page (`/register`) enter your **Full Name**, **Email Address**, and a **Password** (at least 8 characters — a strength meter gives live feedback), confirm the password, and accept the **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy**. Click **Create Account**.

Already registered? Use the **Sign in** link at the bottom of the card.

## Signing in

The **Sign in to your account** page (`/login`) asks for your email and password.

- **Keep me signed in** keeps your session alive across browser restarts.
- **Forgot password?** starts a reset flow: you receive an email with a link to set a new password.
- A caps-lock warning appears while typing your password if Caps Lock is on.

If two-factor authentication is enabled on your account, a second step asks for the **Authentication code** — the 6-digit code from your authenticator app, or one of your recovery codes (`XXXX-XXXX` format). The challenge expires after 5 minutes; use **Back to password** to start over.

## Profile settings

Open the user menu (top right) and choose **Profile** to reach **Profile Settings**.

### Profile information

Click **Edit Profile** to change your **Full Name**, **Location**, **Website**, and **Bio**, then **Save Changes**.

- **Avatar** — **Change avatar** uploads a PNG, JPEG, or WebP image; it is cropped to a square and resized automatically. **Remove** deletes it (your initials are shown instead).
- **Email Address** — changing your email is a verified flow. Click **Change…**, enter the **New email address** and your **Current password**, then **Send confirmation link**. Your current address stays active until you confirm the link sent to the new address; a **Pending verification** banner shows the pending address and lets you **Cancel** the change.

### Password

Click **Change Password**, enter your **Current Password**, a **New Password** (at least 8 characters), and the confirmation. Tick **Sign out all other sessions** to revoke every other device at the same time.

### Two-factor authentication

The **Two-factor authentication** card lets you require a 6-digit authenticator code (TOTP) in addition to your password:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start setup">
    Click **Set up two-factor authentication** and confirm your password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the key to your authenticator app">
    The CMS shows a **Secret key** and an `otpauth` link for your authenticator app. Enter the 6-digit code from the app and click **Verify & enable**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your recovery codes">
    Recovery codes are shown **only once**. Each code signs you in one time if you lose the authenticator. Use **Copy all**, store them safely, then confirm with **I saved them — done**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To turn it off, click **Disable…** and confirm your password — this also invalidates your recovery codes.

### Active sessions

The **Active sessions** card lists devices signed in to your account with browser, last-active time, IP address, and sign-in time. The current device is marked **This device**. Click **Sign out** on any other session to revoke it, or **Sign out all other sessions** to revoke everything except the current one.

### Preferences

Preferences are saved to your account and follow you across devices:

- **Theme** — System, Light, or Dark.
- **Default editing language** — preselected as the target language when you open the [Localization Workshop](/cms/localization).
- **Email notifications** — comments & mentions on your works, and product news & updates.

### Account information and danger zone

**Account Information** shows your user ID, creation date, role, and team count. The **Danger Zone** offers:

- **Export my data** — downloads your profile, preferences, team memberships, and your teams' works as JSON.
- **Deactivate account** — signs you out and blocks future sign-ins. Type the confirmation keyword to enable the button. Your content is retained; contact support to reactivate or fully delete it.

## Teams and roles

Works belong to teams. Open the user menu and choose **Team** to manage them. The page lists your teams in a sidebar; select one to see its members.

### Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Full control, including granting the Admin role |
| **Admin** | Can manage team and settings |
| **Editor** | Can edit content |
| **Viewer** | Can view content only |

Owners and admins can change a member's role with the role dropdown in the members table, or remove members. The owner role itself cannot be reassigned from this table.

### Inviting members

Click **Invite Member**, enter an **Email Address**, and pick a **Role**. Tick **Invite several people at once** for bulk mode — paste addresses (one per line, or comma/semicolon separated) or **Upload CSV**; everyone gets the role you select.

Outstanding invitations appear under **Pending Invitations**, where you can **Copy link**, **Resend** (generates a fresh link and expiry), or **Revoke**. Invitees open the link and accept while signed in.

### Payouts & payments

Teams that sell content connect a Stripe merchant account from the **Payouts & payments** card (**Set up payouts with Stripe** opens Stripe's hosted onboarding). This is required before products can be sold — see [Monetization](/cms/monetization).

## The user menu

The avatar button in the top-right corner of every screen opens a menu with **Profile**, **Settings**, **Team**, a **Dark/Light Mode** toggle, a **Condensed/Regular Font** toggle, **Keyboard Shortcuts**, **Help & Support**, and **Logout**.
